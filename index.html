Locus Coeruleus

Locus Coeruleus – miniscule yet mighty.

Deep within the labyrinth of my mind,
Amidst the intricate tangle of neural fibers.
Little piece of shit makes me nervous.

Releasing norepinephrine – my life hell.

You are the one to make me sweat and hyperventilate
when I reach the cashier.
Help me remember things i ought to leave behind,
keep me up when he sleeps.

Yet, without you I would not get far.
No bike ride, no growth, just bell jar.

simple to put the blame on the brain,
"it's not me, it's the chemistry"
if chemistry is all and you not the katalyst
aren't you still a fatalist?
