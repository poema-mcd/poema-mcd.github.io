sonnet of insanity

i’ve walked the mountains of frontal lobe,
i crossed the cerebrospinal fluid sea.
searching for an end on a globe
each step getting closer to insanity.
does my love hide in the amygdala
or is it hidden in the center of my heart?
that is unlikely, this is not a greek drama.
yet science wouldn’t be without art.
why put feelings into equations,
when it is emotions that make us human.
it’s not subtractions that divide nations,
it’s what moves us beyond reason.
and i find myself moved on a perpetual journey,
neither late, neither early.
