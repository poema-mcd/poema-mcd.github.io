immiscible

try washing off lard in a cold river;
it won't leave you –

like a parasyte.

try treating an asymptomatic cancer patient
by disolving sugar, distributed as a vaccine –

impossible, implausible, impervious.

immiscible;
we walk in overlapping circles.
on crossroads we find who's oil,
who's water.

you are lipophilic,
mix well with the viscous goo.

immobile;
it mellows you out.
you will stick to other's
and they will make their way to the river.

I think I'll go for soap.
