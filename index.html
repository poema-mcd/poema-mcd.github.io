clown pants.

Clementine corduroy clown-pants
We walk wearing funny threads
Woven into fabric by a blue bozo.
–
tactile textile becomes intangible impudence.

We buy books made out of bark in the small store of silent fish heads.

We take the pea train from soy sauce station;
Seated snacking on our hardbacks
– mine tastes of sour plums, yours of wild mushrooms.

Woven fingers, head on shoulder;
We admire the serene salmon sky.
